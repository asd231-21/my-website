Why visit the Morija Cultural Festival?

Join a vivid celebration of Lesotho's arts, heritage, and mountain adventures. Our festival invites both local families and international travelers to connect with Basotho culture through live music, traditional crafts, tasty food, and guided cultural tours.

Live Music & Dance

Feel the rhythm of Sesotho songs and join a community circle with marimba, drums, and dance troupes.

Food & Heritage Market

Sample traditional dishes like moroho and papa, and shop for handmade crafts from Basotho artisans.

Scenic Mountain Activities

Take guided walks to historic rock art sites, and learn about the land that shaped Basotho traditions.

Planning your stay?

Morija is easily reached from Maseru (70 km). There are guesthouses and lodges available, and we can help you arrange transport. Email info@morijafest.ls for travel packages.
