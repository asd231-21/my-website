Tickets & Registration

Secure your spot at Lesotho's biggest cultural event. Choose the ticket that suits your trip, and complete the registration form below.

Ticket Options

Day Pass

M160

Access to all daytime activities, workshops, and food stalls. Valid for one day.

Recommended for: Local visitors and families.

Festival Pass

M360

Full access to all festival days, evening concerts, and performances.

Recommended for: Tourists and international guests.

VIP Experience

M720

Includes priority seating, a guided tour, and a welcome gift pack.

Recommended for: Groups and sponsors.

Note: All ticket prices are listed in Lesotho Maloti (M). Payment is accepted via bank transfer and mobile money. After registration, our team will contact you with confirmation details.
