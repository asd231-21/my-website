Festival Programme

Explore the official lineup of activities across the three-day festival. There is something for every kind of visitor.

Daily Schedule

Time Stage / Zone Activity Notes
09:00 Main Stage Opening Ceremony & Welcome Official welcome with dancers and ceremony leader.
10:30 Heritage Zone Basotho Beadwork Workshop Take home a piece of your own design.
12:00 Food Court Cooking Demo (Moroho & Papa) Taste samples after the demo.
14:00 Adventure Corner Guided Mountain Walk Limited spots. Reserve in advance.
16:00 Artist Studio Storytelling & Oral History Local elders share heritage stories.
18:30 Main Stage Evening Concert Showcase Live bands and dance crews.
20:00 Fire Pit Night Tales & Fire Dance Wrap up with a performance under the stars.

Tip: To beat the midday heat, arrive early and grab a shaded spot near the main stage.

Stage Guide

Main Stage

The festival heart: live performances, opening ceremony, and closing acts.

Adventure Corner

Outdoor guided walks and mountain photo tours. Great for families.
